Induction and Installation Service of Rev Nicholus Ndigha

on 17th April 2024 by v

A Joyful Occasion in Jaribuni, Induction and Installation Service of Rev Nicholus Ndigha being one of the youngest Archdeacon in the Anglican Church of Kenya. The Anglican Church of Kenya (ACK) Jaribuni Parish in Malindi Diocese, Kilifi County was filled with joy and celebration as the community officially welcomed Rev. Nicholus Ndigha as their new Archdeacon. The induction service, held on 9th March 2025,was a vibrant display of faith and unity, presided over by the Rt. Rev. Dr.Reuben S.Katite, Bishop of the Diocese of Malindi.

The ceremony was not only a spiritual milestone but also a community affair, drawing dignitaries, clergy, and thousands of faithful. Among the honored guests was the Governor of Kilifi County His Excellency Gideon Maitha Mung'aro, OGW, whose presence underlined the strong partnership between church and local government.

Highlights from the Ceremony

Bishop Katite, in his moving sermon, spoke about the importance of servant leadership, encouraging Ven. Nicholus to lead with humility, wisdom, and love.

The Governor commended the church for its role in shaping the moral fabric of society and pledged continued support for faith-based community development.

Ven. Nicholus, overwhelmed with gratitude, addressed the congregation with heartfelt words:

"I am deeply honored and humbled to serve this community. May we walk this journey together with faith and purpose."

Unity and Celebration

After the official service, the mood turned celebratory with songs, fellowship, and a communal meal. Laughter and joy echoed throughout the grounds as church members, local leaders, and visitors came together to mark this significant occasion.

A New Chapter Begins

The induction of Ven Nicholus is more than a ceremonial eventâ€”it's the beginning of a new chapter for ACK Jaribuni Archdeaconry. With renewed energy and spiritual guidance, the church looks forward to continuing its mission in spreading the Gospel and serving the people of Malindi Diocese and Kilifi County at large.
